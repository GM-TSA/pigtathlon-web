About

Why?

We were originally making this game for a High School competition called TSA (Technology Student Association). We qualified for states in multiple events - getting 2nd for Video Game Design in regionals. However, shortly after, the Pennsylvania state competition was cancelled due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Events like Video Game Design was initially planned to be judged online, however, the National TSA conference was also cancelled soon after with no plans for remote judging.

Not wanting to completely abandon the project, we decided to continute development for a public, paid, release. We hope you will enjoy playing the game as well as reading and watching our development updates until we release on July 24th, 2020.
