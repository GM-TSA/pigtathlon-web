New Pig Model

We felt that our old pig models weren't working for us and didn't fit the shape we needed them to be to work in some of our minigames and after a few iterations, we decided to choose our rubbery, standing pig model.

Procedural Level Generation

We felt the need to make the game more replayable since now we're going for a full release instead of just a single playthrough by judges. So, we implemented a system where levels could be procedurally generated for our Cross Country minigame.
